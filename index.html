Be

Enjoy everything being OnSite unlocks for you.

1 · Choose your trade.

You don’t join as “customer”, you join as trade. Hit your trade, pick your crew and we’ll tune the whole experience – shop, tools and content – to what you actually do on site.

2 · Gear is your badge.

Colours, patches and prints are not “fashion pieces”. They’re a level system: rookie, crew, lead. Every drop is designed to show what you’ve already proven on site, not how many followers you have.

3 · Tools + crew code.

OnSite Tools – inch calculator, hour tracker, job checklist – are part of the membership. Use them, share them with your crew and keep the code: protect the team, own your mistakes, no clowns on site. That’s what it means to be OnSite.
